Why writers should storyboard promo videos with AI

Writers already think like directors: you build tension, stage reveals, and control what the audience sees in their mind. Storyboarding turns that invisible movie into a shareable visual plan — sequential panels that map what the camera will see and help you plan composition, pacing, and key story beats before production.
